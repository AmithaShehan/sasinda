Sasinda Chandula

Mr. W.H. Sasinda Chandula Prabhashana graduated with a First-Class Honours Electrical and Electronic Engineering degree from the University of Jaffna in 2021. He is currently working as a Lecturer on Contract at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering. He is a member of IEEE and an Associate member of IESL. His research interests are Wireless Communications, Antennas and Propagation, Signal Processing, Computer Networks and Radio Astronomy.
